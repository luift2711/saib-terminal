# Flashcards: NNN② Inside Bar (Nến ôm)

Q: What is NNN② Inside Bar?
A: A cluster of 2 consecutive candles, where the High and Low of the preceding candle (mother bar) completely "engulfs" the High and Low of the following candle (child bar). Energy is being compressed.

Q: What should you be careful about when identifying an Inside Bar?
A: If the High of the child bar exceeds the High of the mother bar by even 1 pip, it is NOT an Inside Bar.

Q: What is the trading process with NNN②?
A: Place a Buy Stop at the mother bar's High + 10 pips. Place a Sell Stop at the mother bar's Low - 10 pips. Once one order triggers, CANCEL the other order immediately. Move SL to Break Even when the price runs 1R.
