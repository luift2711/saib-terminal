# Flashcards: Hammer & Shooting Star

Q: What is the structure of a Hammer candle?
A: The lower wick is 2-3 times longer than the body, and the small body is located at the top. Shows sellers were rejected at low prices. Buy signal when at the bottom of a downtrend.

Q: What is the structure of a Shooting Star candle?
A: The upper wick is 2-3 times longer than the body, and the small body is located at the bottom. Shows buyers were blocked at high prices. Sell signal when at the top of an uptrend.

Q: How to enter a trade safely with a Hammer/Shooting Star?
A: Always wait for the next candle to confirm (close beyond the High/Low of the pattern) or use a Buy Stop/Sell Stop order 10 pips away from the wick.
