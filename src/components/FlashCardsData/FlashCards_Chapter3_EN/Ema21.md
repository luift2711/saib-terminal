# Flashcards: NNN③ EMA21

Q: What is the trading signal according to NNN③ EMA21?
A: A candle CLEARLY CLOSES on the other side of the EMA21 (wicks piercing through do not count). Example: Price is below EMA21, a candle closes above EMA21 = Buy Signal.

Q: What is the most ideal setup ("Kiss the EMA")?
A: Wait for the price to close across the EMA21, then pullback to touch the EMA21 once more before bouncing off. This is the entry point with the smallest Stop Loss.

Q: Is EMA21 effective in a Sideways market?
A: No. When the EMA21 is horizontal (flat), the price will cross up and down continuously creating many false signals. Only use it when the EMA21 is clearly sloping up or down.
