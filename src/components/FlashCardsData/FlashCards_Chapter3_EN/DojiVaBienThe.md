# Flashcards: Doji & Variations

Q: What does a Doji Candlestick represent?
A: The Open and Close prices are nearly equal. It signifies absolute stillness and indecision between buyers and sellers. Often a warning sign of a potential reversal.

Q: What is a Dragonfly Doji?
A: A very long lower shadow, with no upper shadow. A strong Bullish signal if it appears at a support level.

Q: What is a Gravestone Doji?
A: A very long upper shadow, with no lower shadow. A strong Bearish signal if it appears at a resistance level.
