# Flashcards: NNN④ Fibonacci

Q: What are the special Fibonacci levels in the NNN system?
A: 0.62, 0.79, 0.88 (Retracement) and -0.27, -0.62 (Extension 127% and 162%).

Q: Where is the ideal Entry zone?
A: The 62% - 79% zone. Wait for a reversal candle to appear in this zone to enter the trade.

Q: Why add the 88% level?
A: For safe Stop Loss placement. Many traders place SL just below the 79% zone, easily getting "stop hunted" when the price wicks down to 88% before reversing.

Q: How to manage trades with Fibonacci Extension?
A: When the price hits TP1 (127%), take 50% profit and move Stop Loss to Break Even. Let the remaining 50% run to TP2 (162%).
