# Flashcards: NNN① Short-body Candle

Q: What are the characteristics of NNN① Short-body Candle?
A: The Close is near the Open, and the body is very small compared to the entire High-Low range. It represents a "tie" between buyers and sellers.

Q: When is a short-body candle most valuable?
A: When it appears AT or NEAR a major Support/Resistance zone. In the middle of nowhere or during sideways markets, it has no value.

Q: What is the trading process with NNN①?
A: Place 2 orders simultaneously: a Buy Stop at High + 10 pips and a Sell Stop at Low - 10 pips. When 1 order triggers, CANCEL the other order IMMEDIATELY.
