# Flashcards: Morning & Evening Star

Q: What does a Morning Star consist of and what is its meaning?
A: A 3-candle cluster: Candle 1 is a long red (Bearish) -> Candle 2 is small/Doji (Exhaustion) -> Candle 3 is a long green (Explosive Bullish). A reliable BULLISH reversal signal.

Q: What does an Evening Star consist of and what is its meaning?
A: A 3-candle cluster: Candle 1 is a long green (Bullish) -> Candle 2 is small/Doji (Exhaustion) -> Candle 3 is a long red (Explosive Bearish). A reliable BEARISH reversal signal.
