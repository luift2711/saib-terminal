# Flashcards: Harami & Three Soldiers

Q: What is a Harami (Inside bar / Pregnant mother)?
A: The following candle (child) is completely contained WITHIN the body of the preceding candle (mother). Indicates the trend is gradually weakening (accumulating). Weaker than the Engulfing pattern. Often used as a warning to take profit.

Q: What are the Three White Soldiers?
A: 3 consecutive bullish (green) candles, with long bodies and short wicks. The close of each subsequent candle is higher than the previous one. Indicates buyers are completely dominating. A very strong trend confirmation signal.
