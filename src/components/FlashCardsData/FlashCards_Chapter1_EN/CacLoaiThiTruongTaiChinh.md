# Flashcards: Types of Financial Markets

Q: What is Forex?
A: The foreign exchange market (buying and selling currencies of different countries). Example: EUR/USD means using USD to buy EUR. Open 24/5, the largest liquidity in the world.

Q: Characteristics of the Gold market (XAU/USD)?
A: Trades 24/5, very high liquidity, low spread, influenced by inflation, USD, and geopolitics. Suitable for beginners.

Q: What are the 4 main trading sessions in Forex?
A: Sydney, Tokyo (Low volatility), London (Active), New York (Most active). The overlapping period of London & NY (7 PM - 11 PM VN time) is when liquidity is at its peak.

Q: What time of day should you avoid trading Forex?
A: From 12:00 AM - 5:00 AM (VN time). This is a "dead" period, with wide spreads, low volatility, and prone to traps.
