# Flashcards: Who is participating in the market?

Q: Who has the biggest influence on the market?
A: Central Banks (Fed, ECB,...). They decide interest rates and monetary policy, accounting for ~35% of trading volume.

Q: Who is "Smart Money"?
A: Large financial institutions, investment funds (Goldman Sachs, JPMorgan,...). They trade huge volumes and dominate the market.

Q: Who are Retail Traders?
A: You and I. The largest group in number but accounting for the smallest volume (<2%). Our orders do not affect the market.

Q: How do Smart Money liquidity traps work?
A: Smart Money needs large liquidity to buy/sell. They often push prices past support/resistance to trigger Retail Traders' Stop Loss or Breakout orders, then reverse the price.
