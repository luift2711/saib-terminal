# Flashcards: What is a Market?

Q: What is a financial market?
A: It is a place where buyers and sellers meet to exchange financial products (currencies, stocks, gold, crypto). Prices are determined by supply and demand.

Q: Why do prices change?
A: Due to the law of supply and demand. More people want to buy than sell → Price increases. More people want to sell than buy → Price decreases.

Q: What is the "Buy the rumor, sell the news" principle?
A: The market always reflects future expectations, not current reality. When good news actually comes out, those who bought earlier take profit, causing prices to fall.
