# Flashcards: Neuroscience & The 7 Demons

Q: What is the difference between System 1 and System 2 (Kahneman)?
A: System 1 (The Beast): Emotional, automatic, extremely fast. Controls 95% of decisions. System 2 (Rationality): Logical, analytical, slow. In trading, System 1 often "hijacks" before System 2 can analyze.

Q: What does Prospect Theory explain?
A: The pain of losing $100 is 2-2.5 times greater than the joy of making $100. This causes traders to hold onto losers (to avoid pain) and cut winners short.

Q: How to break the bad habit loop in trading?
A: Create a "Pause" between the Cue and the Response. This gives System 2 time to activate instead of acting on the instincts of System 1.
