# Flashcards: Trading Journal

Q: Why is it necessary to keep a Trading Journal?
A: Measurement = Awareness. A Journal is a mirror reflecting a trader's soul, helping you find your mistake "patterns" and identify the 7 demons (e.g., often losing on Mondays, or revenge trading after 2 losses).

Q: In the Journal, do you need to record losing trades that violated the system?
A: MANDATORY. Light kills parasites. Recording mistakes helps the brain become aware of bad behaviors. Pay special attention to recording your EMOTIONS at the time.

Q: What is the most important metric when reviewing the Journal initially?
A: Measure the "Compliance Score" (adherence to the system), NOT the Profit & Loss (P&L). Taking 20 trades where 18 stick to the plan = Huge success.
