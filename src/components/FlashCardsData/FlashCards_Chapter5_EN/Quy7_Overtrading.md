# Flashcards: Demon 7 - Overtrading

Q: What is the nature of Overtrading?
A: Addiction to the thrill (dopamine) when preparing to enter a trade. Turns boredom into throwing money out the window slowly (spreads, commissions, noisy setups).

Q: What are the symptoms of Overtrading?
A: Feeling restless when no trade is open. Entering a trade with small size "for fun" when there is no setup. Taking more trades in a day than the system allows.

Q: What is the Overtrading Exorcism Ritual?
A: Reverse Quota. Set a MAXIMUM number of trades per day (e.g., max 2 trades). Write in the Journal: "Today I refused to enter a trade because the setup wasn't perfect" - this is a huge victory for discipline.
