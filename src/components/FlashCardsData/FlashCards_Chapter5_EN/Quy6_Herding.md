# Flashcards: Demon 6 - Herding

Q: Why is Herding dangerous in trading?
A: Evolutionary instinct makes humans feel safe when following the crowd. But in the market, when the entire crowd buys (thanks to news/KOL tips), it's usually the Top, and vice versa.

Q: What are the symptoms of Herding?
A: Buying/selling based on hyped messages in group chats, Telegram. Changing personal analysis just because an idol says otherwise.

Q: What is the Herding Exorcism Ritual?
A: The Silence Rule. Turn off all groups and news for 30 minutes before analyzing the chart. You must analyze INDEPENDENTLY. "If you need others to confirm your trade, you are just hoping, not trading."
