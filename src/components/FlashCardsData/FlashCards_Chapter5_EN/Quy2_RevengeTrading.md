# Flashcards: Demon 2 - Revenge Trading

Q: How dangerous is Revenge Trading?
A: It's the demon with the fastest account destruction speed. When angry over a loss, the Amygdala (emotional brain) takes over, you lose all logic and often place large trades to "win it back" immediately.

Q: What are the symptoms of Revenge Trading?
A: Immediately opening a new trade right after hitting a Stop Loss. Increasing volume after a losing trade. Feeling the market is "purposely" hunting your SL.

Q: What is the Revenge Trading Exorcism Ritual?
A: Apply the 24h Circuit Breaker Rule. If you lose beyond the daily limit (e.g., 3-5%) -> Close the platform immediately. Never try to "win it back" on the same day.
