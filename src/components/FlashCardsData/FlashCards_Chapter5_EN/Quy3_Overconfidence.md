# Flashcards: Demon 3 - Overconfidence

Q: When does Overconfidence usually appear?
A: Typically after a streak of WINNING trades. The brain illusions that "I control the market", leading to subjectivity and breaking discipline.

Q: What are the symptoms of Overconfidence?
A: Arbitrarily increasing trade size against the plan. Skipping the checklist. Not setting a Stop Loss because "this trade is definitely a winner".

Q: What is the Overconfidence Exorcism Ritual?
A: The Feynman Test. After a winning streak, you must logically explain WHY you won. If it's just a "feeling", it's luck, not skill. Always maintain a fixed size.
