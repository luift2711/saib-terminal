# Flashcards: Demon 1 - FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)

Q: What is FOMO?
A: The fear of being "left behind" when seeing others making money or the price pumping. Causes traders to buy the top or sell the bottom without a setup.

Q: What are the symptoms of FOMO?
A: Buying after the price has surged heavily. Rushing into a trade without checking the checklist. Increasing position size when seeing friends/KOLs showing off profits.

Q: What is the FOMO Exorcism Ritual?
A: When feeling anxious to enter, take a pen and write: (1) Why do I want to enter? (2) What is the technical setup? Writing activates System 2. Always remember: The next opportunity always comes.
