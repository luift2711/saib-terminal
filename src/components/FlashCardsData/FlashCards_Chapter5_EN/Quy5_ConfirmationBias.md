# Flashcards: Demon 5 - Confirmation Bias

Q: What is Confirmation Bias?
A: When you ALREADY decided to buy/sell in your head, then open the chart and only see the signals that "support" that decision, ignoring all risk signals.

Q: What are the symptoms of Confirmation Bias?
A: Analyzing the chart after having decided the direction. Only reading news or listening to KOLs that support your side. Dismissing opposing signals as "just noise".

Q: What is the Confirmation Bias Exorcism Ritual?
A: Play Devil's Advocate. If you want to BUY, force yourself to find at least 2 logical reasons to SELL. If you can't, you are not being objective.
