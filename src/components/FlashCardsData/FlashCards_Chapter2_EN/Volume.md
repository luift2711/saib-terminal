# Flashcards: Volume

Q: What is the role of Volume in technical analysis?
A: Volume is the "evidence" that confirms price action. A price movement accompanied by high volume proves there is real participation from the crowd or Smart Money.

Q: What are the volume signs of a Fake Breakout?
A: Price breaks through resistance/support but with very low volume. This is a sign that few people are participating and the breakout is likely to fail.

Q: What are the volume signs of a confirmed Breakout?
A: Price breaks strongly accompanied by unusually high volume, proving that large amounts of money are pouring in.

Q: What is a "Selling Climax"?
A: A very sharp price drop in a short period accompanied by extremely high volume due to panic selling by the crowd. It often signals that a short-term bottom is about to form.
