# Flashcards: Trend & Structure

Q: How is an Uptrend defined?
A: Higher High (HH) and Higher Low (HL). Strategy: Only look for BUY opportunities at the HL.

Q: How is a Downtrend defined?
A: Lower High (LH) and Lower Low (LL). Strategy: Only look for SELL opportunities at the LH.

Q: What is BOS (Break of Structure)?
A: It's when the price breaks the previous high/low in the direction of the main trend. It signals that the current trend will continue.

Q: What is CHoCH (Change of Character)?
A: It's the first sign that the trend structure has been broken (e.g., an Uptrend breaks the nearest HL). This is an early reversal warning; caution is needed, and Stop Loss should be tightened.
