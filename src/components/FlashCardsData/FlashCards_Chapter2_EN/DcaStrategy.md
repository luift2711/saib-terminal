# Flashcards: DCA Strategy

Q: What is DCA (Dollar Cost Averaging)?
A: Dollar Cost Averaging. You divide your capital and invest regularly at fixed intervals (weekly, monthly) regardless of price fluctuations.

Q: What is the biggest benefit of DCA?
A: It eliminates the psychological pressure of "timing the market." It helps average down the cost basis to a safe level in the long term.

Q: Who is DCA most suitable for?
A: Beginners, those with limited time to monitor charts, or individuals looking to accumulate long-term assets safely and disciplined.

Q: What is the difference between DCA and Active Trading?
A: DCA is a passive method that doesn't require extensive technical analysis. Active Trading demands skills, time spent monitoring charts, risk management, and extremely strong psychological resilience.
