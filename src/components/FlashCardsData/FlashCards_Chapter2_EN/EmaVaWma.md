# Flashcards: EMA & WMA

Q: What is the main difference between SMA and EMA?
A: SMA calculates the arithmetic mean of all data equally (lots of lag). EMA gives higher weight to the most recent prices, allowing it to react faster to the market.

Q: What is the classic 3 EMA combination used to identify trends?
A: EMA21 (Short-term), EMA50 (Medium-term), EMA200 (Long-term).

Q: What are the signs of a "Strong Uptrend" based on EMA?
A: Price is above EMA21, and the order of the lines is: EMA21 > EMA50 > EMA200. All lines are sloping upwards. Strategy: Buy when price pulls back to EMA21.

Q: What are Golden Cross and Death Cross?
A: Golden Cross: Short-term EMA (21) crosses ABOVE long-term EMA (50) → Bullish Signal.
Death Cross: Short-term EMA crosses BELOW long-term EMA → Bearish Signal.
