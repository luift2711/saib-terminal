# Flashcards: Support & Resistance

Q: What is Support?
A: It's the "floor" of the price. A price zone where demand (buyers) is strong enough to prevent the price from falling further, pushing it back up.

Q: What is Resistance?
A: It's the "ceiling" of the price. A price zone where supply (sellers) is strong enough to prevent the price from rising higher, pushing it back down.

Q: What is the "Role Reversal" principle?
A: When a resistance level is broken, it often becomes a new support level. Conversely, when support is broken, it becomes new resistance.

Q: How to identify the strongest Support/Resistance?
A: Use old highs/lows on higher timeframes (D1, Weekly). Round number zones (e.g., BTC $100k) or price zones that have reacted multiple times (>= 3 times).
