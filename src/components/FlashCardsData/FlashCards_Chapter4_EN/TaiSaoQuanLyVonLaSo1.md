# Flashcards: Why is Risk Management #1?

Q: Why is risk management more important than technical analysis?
A: A high win rate does not guarantee profits. If losses are too large on each trade, you can still blow your account. Risk management helps you survive long enough to make money.

Q: What % increase is needed to break even after a 50% account loss?
A: You need a 100% increase to get back to the break-even point. The math of losses is asymmetrical and extremely dangerous.

Q: What are the 3 most common ways traders blow their accounts?
A: 1. Risking too much per trade. 2. Not setting a Stop Loss. 3. Revenge trading (entering large trades to recoup losses).
