# Flashcards: Risk Appetite & Risk Profile

Q: What is a Risk Profile?
A: The level of risk you are willing to accept in exchange for returns. A suitable profile helps you trade comfortably, make clear decisions, and avoid stress.

Q: Who is the "Conservative" profile suitable for?
A: Suitable for beginners (≤1 year) or large capitals that cannot afford to lose. Risk/trade is only 0.5-1%, max Drawdown is 10%. Puts capital preservation first.

Q: What is the "Moderate" profile?
A: It is the international standard for most traders (with 6+ months of experience). It balances protection and growth. Risk/trade is 1-2%, accepting a 20% Drawdown.
