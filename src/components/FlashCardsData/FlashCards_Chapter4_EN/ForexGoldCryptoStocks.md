# Flashcards: Forex / Gold / Crypto / Stocks

Q: What are the risk characteristics of the Crypto market?
A: Extremely high volatility. Stop Loss must be set MUCH WIDER (3-8% of price). High leverage (>5x) should be avoided, and position sizes must be kept small.

Q: What are the characteristics of the Forex and Gold markets?
A: Forex has the highest stability and great liquidity. Gold is heavily affected by news (NFP, Interest rates) and the USD, and can move 20-50 points in a blink of an eye. Beware of weekend gap risks.

Q: What are the characteristics of the Stock market (Especially VN-Index)?
A: Risks of overnight gaps and earnings season gaps. Additionally, the Vietnamese market has a T+3 (T+2.5) rule which ties up capital, preventing immediate reaction.
