# Flashcards: Fixed Fractional & Kelly

Q: What is the Fixed Fractional method?
A: Always risking a fixed percentage based on the CURRENT ACCOUNT BALANCE. When the account grows, the risked amount increases. When the account drops, the risked amount automatically decreases, helping protect capital.

Q: What is the Kelly Criterion?
A: A mathematical formula that calculates the optimal % of capital to risk per trade to maximize long-term account growth, based on Win Rate and R:R.

Q: Why shouldn't you use Full Kelly?
A: Full Kelly yields the highest returns but comes with massive account volatility (50-70% Drawdown), causing heavy psychological pressure. Half Kelly (1/2) or Quarter Kelly (1/4) should be used for safety.
