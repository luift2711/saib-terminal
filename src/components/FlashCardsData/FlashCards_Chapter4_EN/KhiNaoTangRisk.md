# Flashcards: When to increase risk?

Q: What conditions must be met to increase Risk?
A: 1. Traded consistently for at least 3 months. 2. Have at least 50 trades in the Journal. 3. System has a consistent positive expectancy. 4. Actual Max Drawdown is within the acceptable limit. (MUST HAVE ALL 4).

Q: What is Anti-Martingale?
A: Playing big when winning, playing small when losing. When winning, slightly increase size (compounding profits). When losing, reduce size to protect capital.

Q: What is the safe roadmap for increasing risk?
A: Beginners: 0.5 - 1%. Experienced (6-12 months): 1.5 - 2%. Professionals: 2 - 3%. Never risk more than 5%/trade.
